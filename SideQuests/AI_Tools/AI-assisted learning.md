## Why This Supports the Main Quest

AI can explain, quiz, generate practice variants, and reveal blind spots.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Asks random questions
- 1: Uses AI for explanations
- 2: Uses AI for practice and correction
- 3: Builds study prompts for specific courses
- 4: Uses AI to compare methods and assumptions
- 5: Uses AI as a disciplined research assistant
