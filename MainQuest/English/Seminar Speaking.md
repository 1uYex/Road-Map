# Seminar Speaking

## Role

The ability to ask, explain, and think aloud in academic settings.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Can introduce a topic briefly
- 1: Can ask prepared questions
- 2: Can explain a solved problem
- 3: Can give a short technical presentation
- 4: Can handle questions
- 5: Can discuss research direction
- 6: Can communicate as a junior researcher
