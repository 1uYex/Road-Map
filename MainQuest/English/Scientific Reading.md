# Scientific Reading

## Role

The ability to enter textbooks, papers, lecture notes, and documentation.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Can recognize scientific vocabulary
- 1: Can read textbook paragraphs slowly
- 2: Can follow standard textbook sections
- 3: Can extract definitions, assumptions, and claims
- 4: Can read papers selectively
- 5: Can compare papers and identify research taste
- 6: Can read research literature as a working tool
