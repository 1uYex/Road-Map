# Academic Listening

## Role

Access to lectures, seminars, and research talks.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Can catch isolated terms
- 1: Can follow slow educational videos
- 2: Can follow standard lectures with notes
- 3: Can summarize a lecture accurately
- 4: Can track arguments and equations together
- 5: Can ask meaningful follow-up questions
- 6: Can participate in research seminars
