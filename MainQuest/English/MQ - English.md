# MQ - English

English is the communication and research access layer.

## Path

1. [[IELTS]]
2. [[Academic Listening]]
3. [[Scientific Reading]]
4. [[Scientific Writing]]
5. [[Seminar Speaking]]
6. [[Podcast Comprehension]]

## Unlock Logic

- IELTS builds baseline academic mobility.
- Scientific Reading unlocks textbooks and papers.
- Academic Listening and Podcast Comprehension unlock lectures and seminars.
- Scientific Writing and Seminar Speaking unlock research participation.
