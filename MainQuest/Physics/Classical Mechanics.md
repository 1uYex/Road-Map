# Classical Mechanics

## Role

The first training ground for physical modeling, equations of motion, and variational thinking.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Heard of Newtonian mechanics
- 1: Can solve simple force problems
- 2: Can follow undergraduate coursework
- 3: Can solve standard Lagrangian/Hamiltonian problems
- 4: Understands symmetries, conservation laws, phase space
- 5: Has intuition for dynamics and variational principles
- 6: Reads advanced mechanics and theoretical physics with confidence
