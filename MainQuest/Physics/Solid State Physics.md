# Solid State Physics

## Role

A rich bridge between quantum mechanics, many-body thinking, computation, and real materials.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Heard of crystals and bands
- 1: Understands lattice and reciprocal space vocabulary
- 2: Can follow coursework
- 3: Can solve standard band, phonon, and Fermi gas problems
- 4: Understands symmetry, quasiparticles, transport basics
- 5: Connects theory to computational material methods
- 6: Reads research papers in a focused subarea
