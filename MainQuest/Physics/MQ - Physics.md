# MQ - Physics

Physics is the world model.

## Path

1. [[Classical Mechanics]]
2. [[Electrodynamics]]
3. [[Thermodynamics]]
4. [[Statistical Mechanics]]
5. [[Quantum Mechanics]]
6. [[Solid State Physics]]
7. [[Quantum Field Theory]]

## Unlock Logic

- Classical Mechanics develops modeling instincts.
- Electrodynamics develops field thinking.
- Thermodynamics and Statistical Mechanics develop macro-micro reasoning.
- Quantum Mechanics requires Linear Algebra and mathematical maturity.
- Solid State connects quantum/stat mech/computation.
- Quantum Field Theory remains hidden until the foundations are strong.
