# Probability

## Role

The mathematics of uncertainty, ensembles, measurement, noise, markets, and learning systems.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Heard of probability distributions
- 1: Can compute basic probabilities
- 2: Can follow coursework
- 3: Can solve expectation, variance, distribution, and limit theorem problems
- 4: Understands conditional probability, random variables, stochastic thinking
- 5: Connects probability to statistical mechanics and modeling
- 6: Reads research using probabilistic methods
