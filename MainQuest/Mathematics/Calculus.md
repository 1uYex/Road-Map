# Calculus

## Role

The first engine for change, motion, fields, and approximation.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Heard of limits, derivatives, integrals
- 1: Can compute standard derivatives and integrals
- 2: Can handle university coursework
- 3: Can solve standard physics calculus problems
- 4: Understands approximation, series, gradients, multiple integrals
- 5: Sees calculus as local-to-global reasoning
- 6: Uses calculus naturally in advanced mechanics and field theory
