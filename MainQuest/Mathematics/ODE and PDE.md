# ODE and PDE

## Role

Differential equations describe the evolution of systems and fields.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Heard of differential equations
- 1: Can solve basic separable and linear ODEs
- 2: Can follow a standard course
- 3: Can solve standard mechanics, waves, heat, and diffusion problems
- 4: Understands boundary conditions, eigenfunction expansions, Green functions
- 5: Builds physical intuition from equation structure
- 6: Uses PDE language in research-level physics and simulations
