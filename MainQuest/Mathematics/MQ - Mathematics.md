# MQ - Mathematics

Mathematics is the language of the map.

## Path

1. [[Calculus]]
2. [[Linear Algebra]]
3. [[ODE and PDE]]
4. [[Complex Analysis]]
5. [[Real Analysis]]
6. [[Probability]]
7. [[Numerical Methods]]
8. [[Functional Analysis]]

## Unlock Logic

- Calculus + Linear Algebra unlock most early physics.
- ODE/PDE unlock mechanics, electromagnetism, waves, and simulations.
- Complex Analysis supports quantum mechanics, electrodynamics, and theoretical methods.
- Real Analysis and Functional Analysis are late-game foundations.
- Probability supports statistical mechanics, quantum measurement, AI, and quant side quests.

## Current Focus Slot

Only 1-2 math nodes should be active at once.
