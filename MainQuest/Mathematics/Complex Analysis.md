# Complex Analysis

## Role

A bridge between geometry, analysis, waves, quantum mechanics, and advanced theoretical tools.

## Mastery Tiers

- 0: Heard of complex functions
- 1: Understands complex numbers and basic functions
- 2: Can follow a course
- 3: Can solve contour integral and residue problems
- 4: Understands analyticity and singularity structure
- 5: Sees physical meaning in poles, branch cuts, and analytic continuation
- 6: Uses complex methods in advanced theory papers
