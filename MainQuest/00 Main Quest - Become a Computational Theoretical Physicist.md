# Become a Computational / Theoretical Physicist

## Core Statement

Build the mathematical depth, physical intuition, computational skill, and scientific communication ability required for long-term research in theoretical or computational physics.

## Three Permanent Lines

- [[MQ - Mathematics]]
- [[MQ - Physics]]
- [[MQ - English]]

## Mastery Definition

You are progressing when you can:

- solve harder problems with less panic
- read more serious material with more structure
- connect equations to physical meaning
- use computation to test intuition
- explain ideas in English clearly

## Current Strategic Rule

Main Quest first. Side quests must feed it.
