## Folder Roles

- `MainQuest`: permanent growth path.
- `SideQuests`: useful support systems with strict activation limits.
- `Hidden`: future research maturity, mostly locked.
- `DailyLogs`: low-friction evidence trail.
- `ResearchIdeas`: seeds for future research taste.
- `Maps`: visual world maps.
- `00_System`: rules, templates, dashboards, and maintenance protocol.
